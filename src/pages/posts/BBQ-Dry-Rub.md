# BBQ Dry Rub

# Ingredients:
- 2 parts sugar
- 1 part salt
- 1/4 part paprika
- A small amount of chili flakes

# Instructions:
1. Toast the paprika and chili flakes in a pan until fragrant. 
2. Mix all ingredients together and store in an airtight container.
